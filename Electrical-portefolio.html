My college Electrical engineering projects made in the laboratory appear here, as do other projects relating to my major but made outside the laboratory.

Other types of projects displayed here involve Arduino, Arduino shields, IoT projects, and more.

My first steps in learning

The first time I learned about electronics and Arduino was in 2019(10th grade), when I studied Ohm's law, basic parallel resistor schematics and other topics of physics.

As time went on I bought my first Arduino in late 2020 and didn't start doing serious projects until the summer of 2021. As I was finishing high school and headed to college.

My interest in electronics led me to do projects and buy components. As well as learning more about how simple electronics devices can solve real life problems and in gaining experience that may be useful in university.
